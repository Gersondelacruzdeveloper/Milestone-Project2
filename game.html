Instructions

How good is your memory?

Start the game by clicking a card. The card flips and an image of Harry Potter character displays. Click a different card and see if the images match. There are always two identical images somewhere in the game. If the images match, the cards stay flipped. If the images donâ€™t match, both cards are flipped back over and you can select different cards. You can exit the game at any time by clicking the Exit button on the screen or you can restart the game. Can you remember where the matching card is? Once you have found all the matching cards you can either continue on to the next level, play again or you can exit the game. Currently there are 3 levels with more levels coming soon! Enjoy!
